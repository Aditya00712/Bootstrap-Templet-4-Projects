The New Koenigsegg Jesko Attack: Unleashing the Beast

A hypercar designed to break records and redefine performance.

Introducing the Koenigsegg Jesko Attack

The Koenigsegg Jesko Attack is the latest masterpiece from Koenigsegg, engineered to deliver unmatched speed, power, and performance. As a successor to the Agera RS, the Jesko Attack is designed to be a track-focused beast capable of breaking records and pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering.

Unmatched Performance

The Jesko Attack is powered by a 5.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine that produces a mind-blowing 1,600 horsepower and 1,106 lb-ft of torque when running on E85 biofuel. The car features a unique nine-speed multi-clutch Light Speed Transmission (LST), allowing for ultra-fast gear changes and seamless power delivery.

Advanced Aerodynamics

With its state-of-the-art aerodynamic design, the Jesko Attack generates over 1,400 kg of downforce at 250 km/h (155 mph). The car features a massive rear wing, front splitter, and advanced underbody airflow management to maximize grip and stability on the track.

"The Jesko Attack is a testament to our relentless pursuit of automotive perfection."

Christian von Koenigsegg, CEO of Koenigsegg

Interior and Technology

Inside, the Jesko Attack offers a blend of luxury and cutting-edge technology. The interior features premium materials, a digital instrument cluster, and a state-of-the-art infotainment system. Advanced driver assistance systems and customizable driving modes ensure a thrilling and safe driving experience.
